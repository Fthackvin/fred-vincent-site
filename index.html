About

I was founding designer at Kaizen, a marathon training app I helped build from scratch. Before that I led product design at Gastfreund, taking a "Slack for hotels" from concept to a multi-product suite. I've worked across consumer and B2B, with small teams and 40-person ones, on products people use every day and on tools they only see once.

Alongside the design work, I keep a parallel practice as a maker, painting, ceramics, the occasional small object. It's the same activity in a different medium. Both are about finding the version of the thing that's worth making.

I'm based in Berlin.

Available for contract and fractional work.

Open to founders and product teams who want help finding the version of the work that's worth committing to.
